About Me

Hi! My name is Parisa Hariri. I'm a Computer Science student at the University of San Diego, graduating in May 2026. I currently work remotely as a Software Engineer I Technical Resident at Abbott Diabetes Care on the Cloud Platform Team, where I develop backend applications in Go and learn about Kubernetes and cloud-native systems.

Previously, I interned at Medtronic Diabetes as a DevOps Engineer Intern, improving CI/CD pipeline efficiency and supporting AWS-based deployments. These experiences strengthened my skills in software development and infrastructure while growing my appreciation for how technology can make a real-world impact in healthcare.

Leadership & Involvement

My involvement in SWE has taught me how to lead collaboratively, manage large-scale events, and empower other women in engineering.
